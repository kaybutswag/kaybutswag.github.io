About Me

Before diving into the depths of web development, I worked as an economic litigation consultant ready to make a career change. Translation: I transformed multiple forms of messy data into a story through analyses and regressions. Along the way, I had glimpses of powerful tools just out of my reach: data scraping, API's, and machine learning. I envied the power to create. After three and a half years, I decided I was ready to make a change.

Beyond an enhancement of my data experience, I wanted to understand the tools I took for granted, from Data to Design. My projects are the products of that desire. I primarily code in Javascript, recently focusing on React. Also in my toolbox, JQuery, HTML, CSS, Mongo, Python, SAS and Handlebars. As of now, I have more Frontend experience, but adding to my Backend resume regularly. Look around and let me know what you think!
