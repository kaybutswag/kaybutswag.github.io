About Me

Before diving into the depths of web development, I was an economic litigation consultant ready to make a career change. If you're one of the many who don't understand my job title, think about hearing that a company is being sued for $3.2 million. Somebody had to calculate that number and defend it against fierce opposition. Well, that was my world. I gathered, cleaned, and analyzed that data, preparing it for the story it was going to tell.

Along the way, I had glimpses of powerful tools just out of my reach: data scraping, API's, and machine learning. I envied the power to create. After three and a half years, I decided I was ready to make a change. I wanted more than an enhancement of my data experience, but to see how tools I took for granted were made, from data to design. My site and projects are the products of that desire. Look around and let me know what you think!
